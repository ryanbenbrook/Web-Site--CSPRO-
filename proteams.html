Professional Teams

Counter-Strike has become so a large competitive game that it would be impossible to list every "professional" team out there. We are just going to focus on the most popular teams that have seen the most success. While these teams may not necessarily be in their prime currently, they have an impressive history. Another important thing to note is that a lot of these teams are composed of members that may have once played with each other and moved on to other rosters.
